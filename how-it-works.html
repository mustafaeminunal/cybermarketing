How WhiskAWish Works

Making a difference in cats' lives has never been easier. Learn how our platform connects caring people with cats in need.

1

Choose a Cat

Browse through our network of shelter and stray cats. Each profile includes the cat's story, photos, and feeding schedule. You can filter by location, feeding urgency, or specific needs.

2

Make a Donation

Select the amount you'd like to contribute. Each meal costs $2, and you can sponsor multiple meals or set up recurring donations. 100% of your donation goes directly to feeding cats.

3

Watch Live

Once your donation is processed, you'll receive a notification when your sponsored cat is about to be fed. Watch the feeding live through our camera system and see the immediate impact of your kindness.

4

Track Your Impact

Follow your impact through our dashboard. See how many meals you've sponsored, receive updates about your favorite cats, and connect with other donors making a difference.
