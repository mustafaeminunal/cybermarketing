Luna's Journey: From Street Life to Forever Home

March 20, 2024 Success Story

When we first met Luna on the streets of Bucure»ôti, she was a shy, undernourished grey cat who would barely let anyone approach her. Found huddled in a corner of an old building, Luna's story is a testament to the power of patience, love, and dedication.

The Beginning

Our volunteers first spotted Luna in December 2023, during one of our regular feeding rounds. Despite her initial fear, there was something special about her gentle eyes that told us she wanted to trust humans again.

The Transformation

Over several weeks, our dedicated team worked to gain Luna's trust. Through consistent feeding and gentle interaction, we slowly broke down her barriers. The breakthrough came when she finally allowed our volunteer Maria to pet her head - a moment that brought tears to everyone's eyes.

Medical Care and Recovery

Once we were able to rescue Luna, our veterinary team discovered she had several health issues that needed attention. Through proper medical care and nutrition, Luna's physical health improved dramatically, matched only by her blossoming personality.

Finding Her Forever Home

After two months of rehabilitation, Luna was ready for adoption. The Popescu family fell in love with her gentle nature and took her home. Today, Luna is unrecognizable from the scared street cat we first met. She's now a confident, loving companion who enjoys window watching and playing with her toy mice.

Luna's Journey in Numbers:

  • 2 months of rehabilitation
  • Full medical treatment and vaccination
  • Gained 2.5kg to reach healthy weight
  • Now living happily with her forever family
