Health & Safety Guidelines

Learn how to identify health issues and provide basic care for stray cats.

Common Health Issues

Learn to recognize these common health problems in stray cats:

  • Upper respiratory infections (URI)
  • Fleas and parasites
  • Wounds and injuries
  • Malnutrition and dehydration
  • Dental problems

Warning Signs

Watch for these signs that indicate a cat needs immediate medical attention:

  • Difficulty breathing or labored breathing
  • Severe lethargy or unresponsiveness
  • Visible wounds or bleeding
  • Signs of pain or distress
  • Not eating for more than 24 hours

Basic First Aid

Essential first aid tips for emergency situations:

  • Keep a first aid kit ready
  • Know local emergency vet contacts
  • Learn safe handling techniques
  • Use proper protective equipment
  • Document injuries and symptoms

Preventive Care

Steps to prevent health issues in stray cat populations:

  • Regular feeding schedule
  • Clean water sources
  • TNR program participation
  • Monitor for new cats or illness
  • Maintain clean feeding areas
