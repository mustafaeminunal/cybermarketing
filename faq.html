Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WhiskaWish and our mission to help cats in need.

How does WhiskaWish work?

WhiskaWish connects caring individuals with cats in need across Romania. You can donate to provide meals, view feeding stations on our interactive map, and track the impact of your contributions in real-time.

How is my donation used?

100% of your donation goes directly to feeding cats. Each $2 donation provides one nutritious meal. We maintain full transparency about how funds are used and provide regular updates about the cats you've helped.

Can I choose which feeding station to support?

Yes! You can select specific feeding stations from our interactive map and direct your donations to support cats in those locations. You'll receive updates about the cats you're helping at your chosen station.

How do I know my donation is making an impact?

We provide real-time updates through our platform, including photos and stories from feeding stations. You can track the number of meals provided and cats helped through your personal impact dashboard.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, WhiskaWish is a registered non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible. You'll receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

How can I volunteer or get involved?

We welcome volunteers! You can help by maintaining feeding stations, documenting cat colonies, or assisting with our social media presence. Contact us through our volunteer form to learn more about opportunities in your area.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact our support team for assistance.
