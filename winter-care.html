Winter Care Guide

Help outdoor cats stay warm and safe during cold weather months.

Building Winter Shelters

Essential tips for creating effective winter shelters:

  • Use waterproof materials
  • Elevate shelters off the ground
  • Make entrance face away from wind
  • Use straw for insulation (not hay or blankets)
  • Size appropriately for body heat retention

Winter Feeding Tips

Special considerations for feeding cats in cold weather:

  • Increase food portions during cold months
  • Use heated water bowls to prevent freezing
  • Place food inside shelters when possible
  • Feed on a strict schedule
  • Provide wet food in insulated bowls

Cold Weather Hazards

Be aware of these winter-specific dangers:

  • Antifreeze poisoning risks
  • Cars seeking warmth in engines
  • Frostbite on ears and paws
  • Dehydration from frozen water
  • Salt and chemical exposure

Emergency Preparedness

How to prepare for winter storms and extreme weather:

  • Create multiple shelter options
  • Stock extra food and supplies
  • Know the signs of hypothermia
  • Have emergency vet contacts ready
  • Monitor weather forecasts
